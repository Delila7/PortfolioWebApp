Power BI Report of Student Retention Factors

This analysis examines factors contributing to student retention at a university. Of the sample of 4,258 students who registered in Fall 2023, 34% did not register in Fall 2024. Findings suggest Sophomores with undeclared majors are at higher risk for not returning to university, which highlights need for more support for students around major declaration.
